## Seasonal Notes
**Spring/Fall Ideal:** DO NOT plan this trip for January or February, as the Miller House & Columbus tours completely shut down for preservation activities. Given the Midwestern summer humidity, targeting April/May or September/October will make wandering Broad Ripple and downtown Columbus infinitely more pleasant. 

## Re-Verification Reminders
- **Check exact tour availability:** Columbus architecture tour times shift by the month (e.g., they only run Fri-Sat in March and December). 
- **Check Bluebeard walk-in times:** If you cannot snag an advance reservation at Bluebeard, verify their current opening hour and arrive 15 minutes prior to get a table right away.
- **Time Zone:** Don't forget Indy is on Eastern Time (EST). You will lose an hour driving there and gain it back driving home.
