# Stops

## Lodging

- **The Edgewater** — lakeside, splurge-tier. Lake-view rooms add ~$60/night and it's worth it. Hotel breakfast is fine; hotel dinner is meh.

## Food (this is the trip)

- **Graze** — Friday dinner. Small plates, cheese-forward. Reserve.
- **Tornado Steak House** — Saturday dinner. Old-school Wisconsin supper club: relish tray, brandy old-fashioneds, perfectly cooked steak. Reservations mandatory.
- **Estrella** — Sunday brunch alternative if the market isn't your thing.
- **Barriques** (multiple locations) — best coffee in town. The Atwood location is nicest to sit at.
- **Babcock Hall Dairy Store** (on UW campus) — university ice cream. The cheese counter is genuinely good.

## Walks

- **State Street** — the main spine. Capitol on one end, UW on the other.
- **Lakeshore Path** (Memorial Union → Picnic Point) — ~3 miles one way along Lake Mendota. Easy and beautiful.
- **Lake Monona Loop** — longer (~13 miles) if you have the legs and rent bikes.

## Other

- **Capitol** — Saturday open hours include the rotunda. Free.
- **Dane County Farmers' Market** — Sunday mornings on the Capitol Square. Get there before 10.
- **Memorial Union Terrace** — the famous chairs. Beer, lake, sunset. Yes.
- **Crystal Corner Bar** (Willy Street) — late-night music in a cozy room. Cash-friendly.
