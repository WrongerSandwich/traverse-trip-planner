# Stops

## In the park

- **Starved Rock Lodge** — base for the weekend. Historic CCC-built (1930s), stone fireplaces, dark wood, an old indoor pool. Restaurant on site for breakfast and dinner. The main lodge rooms are small and rustic; the newer wing is more motel-style.
- **Visitor Center** — pick up the trail map even if you've done it before; trail closures rotate based on canyon conditions.
- **French Canyon → St. Louis Canyon loop** — best for waterfalls. ~3 miles round trip with some scrambling. Falls peak after rain or snowmelt.
- **Council Overhang / Ottawa Canyon / Kaskaskia Canyon** — deeper, quieter canyons farther east. ~4 miles. Lower traffic than the canyons near the lodge.
- **Wildcat Canyon** — the iconic Starved Rock photo (footbridge over a narrow canyon). Easy add-on; crowded at peak.

## Outside the park

- **Utica** (3 mi west) — Duffy's Tavern for a casual dinner if not eating at the lodge. Small downtown, good for a stroll after.
- **LaSalle / Peru** (15 min west) — bigger town if you want a real coffee shop. Tangled Roots has a decent breakfast.
- **Pontiac** (1h south) — Route 66 Museum + walkable downtown with a few murals. Good return-trip stop.
