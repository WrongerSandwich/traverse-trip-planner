# Route

Springfield → Galena is a straight northwest shot of about 215 miles, roughly 3.5 hours.

- **Springfield → Peoria** (I-55 to I-155/US-24): ~1h15m of flat central-Illinois farmland.
- **Peoria → Galena** (US-6 / IL-92 / US-20): the back half climbs into the Driftless — the unglaciated corner where the land finally starts to roll. US-20 west of Freeport is the scenic stretch.

The Galena River valley reveals itself on the final descent into town; Horseshoe Mound, just east on US-20, is worth the five-minute detour for the first big overlook.
