# Logistics

## What we booked ahead

- The Edgewater, lake-view room (booked ~6 weeks out — was tight)
- Graze for Friday dinner
- Tornado Steak House for Saturday dinner

## What we wished we'd booked ahead

- A show at the Overture or the Majestic — both sold out the weekend we visited
- A bike rental on Saturday afternoon — would have been a great way to do the lake loop, but rentals were thin

## What worked

- Three nights felt right. Two would have meant skipping either the farmers' market or the lakeshore walk.
- The Edgewater being walking distance to State Street meant no parking-hunting.
- Pacing: one big-deal meal per day, lighter food between.

## Notes for future Madison trips

- Spring weekends (April–May) are quieter than summer; weather is a coin flip but the lakes are walkable and the restaurants aren't slammed.
- Check the UW academic calendar — graduation weekend is a different city.
- Bring layers; the lakeshore is windier than it looks.
