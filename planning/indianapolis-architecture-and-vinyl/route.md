## Segment 1: Overland Park, KS to St. Louis, MO
- **Distance/Time:** ~250 miles / 3.5 to 4 hours
- **Route:** I-70 E straight across Missouri.
- **Notes:** You'll be taking the RAV4, meaning zero charging stops are required. St. Louis makes a logical middle ground to stretch your legs and grab lunch (recommend finding a bite in St. Charles or near Forest Park rather than navigating deep downtown if you want to keep moving). 

## Segment 2: St. Louis, MO to Indianapolis, IN
- **Distance/Time:** ~243 miles / 3.5 to 4 hours
- **Route:** Continue on I-70 E through Illinois and into Indiana.
- **Notes:** You will lose an hour as you cross into the Eastern Time Zone (Indiana). The Alexander is located directly downtown, just off the interstate. 

## Segment 3: Indianapolis to Columbus, IN (Day Trip)
- **Distance/Time:** ~45 miles / 45-50 minutes (one way)
- **Route:** I-65 S.
- **Notes:** An easy, straight-shot morning drive down from downtown Indy. 

## Segment 4: Downtown to Broad Ripple (Local Transit)
- **Distance/Time:** ~8 miles / 20 minutes (one way)
- **Route:** North via N Meridian St or College Ave.
- **Notes:** Broad Ripple is located on the north side of Indianapolis. Parking in Broad Ripple can be notoriously tight on weekend nights; consider taking a Lyft/Uber from The Alexander if you plan on drinking.
