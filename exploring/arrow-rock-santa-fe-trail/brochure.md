field_guide_notes:
  - Arrow Rock's population hovers under one hundred. On a weekday without a Lyceum show, the main street can be nearly empty — which is its own reward, if you're inclined to stand in the middle of an 1840s outfitting town without anyone else around.
  - The Weinreich Ruts are easier to read in late fall or early spring, when the grass dies back and the indentations left by iron-rimmed wheels in the 1820s and 30s become legible again. Summer visits may find them overgrown and tree-shadowed.
  - The return route on US-24 along the Missouri River's south bank follows, for long stretches, the actual alignment of the Santa Fe Trail — the sensation of driving 'on' rather than 'past' history is worth the extra 45 minutes over I-70.
  - J. Huston Tavern reopened under new nonprofit management after an unexpected closure in early 2024. The food is reported to be strong, but the operation is still maturing — call ahead to confirm hours, especially outside of Lyceum show weekends.
gotchas:
  - There is no gas station in Arrow Rock. Fill up at the I-70 exit (MO-41 / Exit 98) before turning north — it's 13 miles of two-lane highway with no services.
  - Arrow Rock B&Bs are small (often 2–4 rooms each) and fill quickly on Lyceum show weekends. Book lodging before buying theatre tickets, not after.
  - Buy Lyceum tickets only at LyceumTheatre.org or by calling 660-837-3311. Third-party broker sites sell inflated tickets for this venue specifically — the theatre has flagged this as an active problem.
  - J. Huston Tavern is closed Monday through Thursday except on Lyceum show nights. If your visit falls on a non-show weekday, Catalpa (Fri–Sun only) is also unavailable — plan arrival for a show weekend or verify alternatives in advance.
  - The Bolt is marginal for this trip. Arrow Rock has no public charging and village B&Bs are unlikely to offer L2. If using the Bolt, charge to 100% before leaving and plan a stop at Electrify America in Columbia (I-70 Exit 124); the scenic return via Lexington makes charging logistics thinner — default to the RAV4.
