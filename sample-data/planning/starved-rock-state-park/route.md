# Route notes

Straight north from Springfield on I-55, then east at Bloomington onto I-39 / I-80. About 2h15 if traffic cooperates around Bloomington-Normal, a bit longer Friday afternoon.

The interstate is the fast way and not the scenic way. For a slower return on Sunday, drop south off I-80 at Utica onto IL-71, then IL-23 through Streator and Pontiac — Route 66 alignment from Pontiac south is the scenic alternative, with a couple of small-town stops (Pontiac's Route 66 Museum is genuinely good).

Gas one stop is plenty in the CR-V. EV plan would be: top off in Bloomington (Tesla / EVgo cluster off I-55), arrive with ~60% remaining, charge at Utica or at the lodge if they've added L2 since last summer.
