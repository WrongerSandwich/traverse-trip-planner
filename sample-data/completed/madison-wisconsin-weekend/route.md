# Route notes

Springfield → I-55 → Bloomington → I-39 → Rockford → I-90 → Madison.

About 4h15 with one fuel stop. Friday afternoon traffic through Rockford is the only real congestion; left Springfield at 3pm, arrived a bit after 7.

Return on Sunday was cleaner — out of Madison by 11am, home by 3:30. Stopped in Janesville for lunch at Citrus Cafe (better than its strip-mall location suggests).

Next time: try US-51 north of Bloomington instead of I-39 — adds 20 minutes but cuts the worst of the truck traffic and runs through small towns we never see otherwise.
