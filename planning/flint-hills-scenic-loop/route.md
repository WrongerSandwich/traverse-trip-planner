# Route — Flint Hills Tallgrass Prairie Loop

## Overland Park to Council Grove (~80 mi, ~1.25 hrs)

Take I-35 south briefly, then **US-56 west** from Gardner through Ottawa and into Council Grove. The farmland opens progressively as you head west. Council Grove is a legitimate first stop: it was the last outfitting point on the Santa Fe Trail and the historic downtown reflects that. Worth a coffee or lunch at Hays House 1857 and a browse at Flint Hills Books before continuing.

## Council Grove to Cottonwood Falls: Hwy 177 Corridor (~30 mi, ~35 min — allow more)

This is the centerpiece. Turn south onto **Hwy 177** from Council Grove. The landscape opens almost immediately as the road crests the first ridge and the tree cover disappears. Key moments:

- **First ridge south of Council Grove** — the prairie panorama opens here. This is the visual payoff.
- **Township roads east and west of Hwy 177** — most are paved or solid gravel and allow short detours onto ridgelines. No specific signage; turn when you see a view.
- **Tune to 1680 AM** along the corridor for narrated Flint Hills history, burning, and ecology (plays on a loop; works intermittently).
- **Schrumpf Hill Scenic Overlook** (~3 miles south of Cottonwood Falls) — formal pullout with wildflower bed and panoramic view over the Cottonwood River valley. Easy to miss northbound; watch for signage.

## Tallgrass Prairie National Preserve

The Preserve entrance is north of Strong City on **Hwy 177** — watch for the signed turn to the historic ranch area. The visitor center is inside the 1881 limestone Z-Bar/Spring Hill Ranch buildings (mansion, barn, Lower Fox Creek one-room schoolhouse).

Trail system: 40+ miles, ranging from easy front-country loops starting at the ranch to backcountry routes through Windmill Pasture (bison territory). Trails are mowed grass and gravel ranch roads — good walking shoes, no technical gear.

## Optional: Matfield Green (~15 mi south of Cottonwood Falls on Hwy 177)

A near-ghost town of fewer than 50 people with photogenic limestone buildings. Worth a 15-minute side trip to see what the depopulated Flint Hills interior looks like. No services — gas up in Cottonwood Falls first.

## Return Options

- **Direct via I-35 through Emporia** — ~50 min to Emporia, ~1.5 hrs to Overland Park. Fastest.
- **Add Council Grove via US-50 east** — good if you skipped it on the way down.
- **All-177 north to Manhattan then I-70** — adds significant time but completes the full byway.
