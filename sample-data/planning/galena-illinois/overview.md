# Galena Driftless Weekend

Galena packs an improbable amount of preserved 19th-century town into a few blocks of bluff above the river — the whole downtown is a National Historic District, and it's an easy, walkable base for a Friday-to-Sunday escape from Springfield.

The plan keeps the pace gentle: arrive Friday and wander Main Street, give Saturday to the Grant history, a winery, and a sunset overlook, then take one flat river walk on Sunday before heading home. It leans into what travels well here — old hotels, weird-good museums, independent food, and big Driftless views without a real hike.
