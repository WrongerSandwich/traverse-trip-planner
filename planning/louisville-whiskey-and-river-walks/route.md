## Segment 1: Overland Park, KS to St. Louis, MO
* **Distance:** ~250 miles
* **Drive Time:** ~3 hours 45 minutes
* **Details:** Hop on I-70 E crossing Missouri. This is a straightforward interstate burn. The St. Louis area (near Forest Park) makes an excellent natural midpoint to grab a coffee, stretch your legs, or grab a quick bite before crossing the Mississippi River.

## Segment 2: St. Louis, MO to Louisville, KY
* **Distance:** ~260 miles
* **Drive Time:** ~4 hours
* **Details:** Take I-64 E through southern Illinois and Indiana. The route is an easy, mostly flat drive that drops you directly into downtown Louisville. You can pull straight up to the 21c Museum Hotel on West Main Street for valet parking and check-in. Total driving time for the day is roughly 7.5 to 8 hours, making a Thursday morning departure the best move to maximize your weekend.

*Vehicle Note:* Since you are taking the 2019 RAV4, DC fast-charging logistics and range anxiety won't be a factor for this ~500-mile highway stretch.
