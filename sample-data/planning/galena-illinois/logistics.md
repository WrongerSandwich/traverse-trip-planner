# Logistics

**When:** A Friday–Sunday in late summer. Spring and fall are also excellent; deep winter shutters a lot of Main Street.

**Lodging:** DeSoto House Hotel, two nights, right on Main Street so the car can stay parked. The Irish Cottage east of town is the backup if downtown is full.

**Getting around:** Park once and walk. Downtown lots fill by midday on summer weekends — the Bench Street lot above Main usually has space when the riverside ones don't.

**Reservations:** Book the hotel ahead, and reserve Fritz and Frites for Saturday dinner — the dining room is tiny.

**Food rhythm:** Casual lunches on Main Street, one nicer Saturday dinner, a bakery coffee before the Sunday drive.
