## Seasonal & Crowding Notes
* **Weather Warning:** You generally avoid humidity and heat, so taking this trip in the current late-spring into summer (June–August) window isn't recommended. Aim for September or late October instead.
* **Major Crowd Gotcha:** Avoid the **first weekend in October!** Old Louisville hosts the St. James Court Art Show during this weekend. While famous, it brings over 300,000 visitors to the walking courts, causing massive street closures, impossible parking, and intense tourist energy that directly clashes with your preference to avoid high-season crowds. 

## Route & Vehicle specific rules
* Taking the Toyota RAV4 is the play here. 517 miles is a solid day of driving, and minimizing the stops to standard gas fill-ups and food will keep the travel day closer to 8 hours instead of the 10-11 hours that an EV charge strategy would necessitate. 
* Cell coverage on the I-70 to I-64 corridor is perfectly stable.
